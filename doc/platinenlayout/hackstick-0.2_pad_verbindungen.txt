AVR zu MAX232 Pads:
===================

RX_5V <--> DATA0 (R1OUT)
TX_5V <--> DATA1 (T1IN)

Padverbindungen:
Hozizontal
[RX_5V]-[DATA0]
[TX_5V]-[DATA1]

Max232 zu SUB-D9 Pads:
===========================

TX_HV <--> DATA0_HV (R1IN)
RX_HV <--> DATA1_HV (T1OUT)

Padverbindungen:
Horizontal
[DATA0_HV]-[TX_HV]
[DATA1_HV]-[RX_HV]
